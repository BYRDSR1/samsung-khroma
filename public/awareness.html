The simple answer is that a colorblind person should avoid professions where correctly identifying a particular color is critical to their profession and safe for others. Living with Colour Vision Deficiency

Although color blind people do develop strategies to help them cope, shopping for fruit and vegetables can be a real problem. Colour blind people often have to learn that ripe apples are often darker than unripe apples and that generally ripe fruit feels softer than unripe fruit and smells different.
